ABOUT

WeatherAltermath is a website designed to help residents to know about their local wheather and be alert of any upcoming or current wheather alerts in their area. This website is designed to teach you about weather emergencies, know your local emergency numbers, and help you plan for the next weather disaster or event. WeatherAltermath also has links to resources and entertainment options for residents who are currently waiting for instructions from authorities after significant weather events.
